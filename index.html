Brief History on Us

JHB NORTH PLUMBING was established nearly 30 years ago with the goal of delivering reliable, high-quality plumbing solutions to homes and businesses in Johannesburg and surrounding areas. From small residential repairs to large-scale commercial and industrial projects, the company has steadily built a reputation for professionalism, efficiency, and attention to detail. Over the years, JHB North Plumbing has successfully completed major developments in estates such as Featherbrooke, Douglasdale, Eagle Canyon, Waterfall Country Estate, Silver Valley Estate, and Pretoria. The company is also proud to have handled all plumbing installations at The Palm Continental Hotel in Johannesburg. Through consistent service excellence, JHB North Plumbing has grown from a small local business into a trusted name in the plumbing industry, recognized for its personalized service, cost-effective solutions, and commitment to customer satisfaction.
