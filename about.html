Quick Plumbing Tips from the Experts

1. Know Your Main Water Valve

Locate your main shut-off valve and learn how to turn it off in case of an emergency to prevent serious water damage.

2. Never Put Grease Down the Sink

Avoid pouring oils or fats down the drain — wipe pans first to prevent blocked pipes and costly repairs.

3. Be Mindful of Toilet Use

Only flush toilet paper. Wet wipes and hygiene products cause severe drain blockages.

4. Insulate Exposed Pipes

Protect outdoor and unheated pipes with insulation to prevent freezing and bursting during winter.
